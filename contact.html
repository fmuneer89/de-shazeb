Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the minimum order quantity?
MOQ is 50 pieces per design per colour for bulk orders. Retail orders have no minimum — buy 1 piece.
How long does production take?
Retail orders ship in 2–4 working days. Bulk orders take 2–3 weeks production + 2–4 days delivery.
Do you offer private label / custom branding?
Yes — full OEM including custom labels, hangtags and packaging. Contact us for pricing on 50+ pcs.
What payment terms do you offer?
Retail: full payment upfront. Bulk: 50% advance on confirmation, balance before dispatch.
Do you deliver outside Karachi?
Yes — nationwide via TCS and Leopards. Free delivery above Rs 2,000. International orders welcome.
Can I get samples first?
Sample shirts are available. Cost is deducted from your first bulk order. WhatsApp us to arrange.
