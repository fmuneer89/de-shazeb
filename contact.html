Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order is 50 pieces per design per colour. For mixed designs, please contact us to discuss custom arrangements.
How long does production take?
Standard production time is 2–3 weeks from order confirmation and advance payment. We keep you updated at every stage.
Do you offer private label / custom branding?
Yes! We offer full OEM private label including custom woven labels, hangtags, and branded packaging. Contact us for pricing.
What payment terms do you offer?
We require 50% advance on order confirmation, with the remaining 50% due before dispatch. Bank transfer and EasyPaisa accepted.
Do you deliver outside Karachi?
Yes, we deliver nationwide via TCS and Leopards Courier. Free delivery on orders above Rs 2,000. International shipping available on request.
Can I get samples before placing a bulk order?
Sample shirts are available. Sample cost is deducted from your first bulk order. WhatsApp us to arrange sample delivery.
