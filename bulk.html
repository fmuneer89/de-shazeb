Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity (MOQ) is 50 pieces per design per color. For custom manufacturing, MOQ may be higher depending on specifications.

How long does production take?

Standard production takes 2–3 weeks after order confirmation and 50% advance payment. Rush orders may be accommodated — contact us to discuss.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfer, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and online card payments via Snipcart. We require 50% advance to start production and 50% before delivery.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Yes! Sample shirts are available. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange samples. Sample cost is deducted from your bulk order.

Do you offer private label / OEM?

Absolutely. We can manufacture shirts under your brand name with your labels, hang tags, and packaging. Minimum 100 pieces for private label orders.

Do you ship internationally?

We are currently working towards international export. We can discuss international orders — contact us with your requirements and location.

What is your return policy?

We have a 7-day return policy for manufacturing defects. Items must be unused and in original packaging. We replace or refund defective items.

How do I track my order?

After order confirmation, we keep you updated via WhatsApp at every stage — production start, production complete, and dispatch with tracking details.
