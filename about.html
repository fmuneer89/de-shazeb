Our Story

Built on Quality
& Commitment

"Every shirt is a promise of quality delivered."

Junaid Garment was founded in Karachi with a clear mission — to manufacture premium men's formal shirts that meet international quality standards while remaining competitively priced.

Operating from our facility at Plot 210/1, 97 Street 5, Sector I, Manzoor Colony, Karachi, we serve local retailers, wholesale distributors, and international importers.

We take pride in being a trusted supplier to businesses in UAE, UK, USA, Saudi Arabia and beyond — with proper documentation, reliable quality, and on-time delivery.
