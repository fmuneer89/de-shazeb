Our Story

Built on Quality
& Commitment

"Every shirt is a promise of quality delivered."

De-Shazeb was founded with one clear mission — to manufacture premium men's formal shirts that meet high quality standards while remaining competitively priced for businesses.

Operating from our facility at Plot 210/1, 97 Street 5, Sector I, Manzoor Colony, Karachi, we serve local retailers, wholesale distributors, and are working towards international markets.

We take pride in being a trusted supplier to businesses across Pakistan — with reliable quality, on-time delivery, and excellent customer support.
