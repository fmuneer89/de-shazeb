Our Story

"Every shirt is a promise of quality delivered."

De-Shazeb was founded with one clear mission — to manufacture premium men's formal shirts that meet high quality standards while remaining competitively priced for businesses across Pakistan.

Operating from our facility at Plot 210/1, 97 Street 5, Sector I, Manzoor Colony, Karachi, we serve local retailers, wholesale distributors, and are actively working towards international markets.

We take pride in being a trusted supplier to businesses across Pakistan — with reliable quality, on-time delivery, and excellent customer support. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the De-Shazeb promise: craftsmanship you can see, quality you can feel.

Our Journey

How We Grew

2019
De-Shazeb Founded
Established in Manzoor Colony, Karachi with a singular focus on premium men's formal shirts for local retailers.
2020
Wholesale Expansion
Expanded operations to serve wholesale distributors across Karachi and Lahore. Introduced bulk order programme with MOQ 50 pieces.
2022
Nationwide Reach
Launched nationwide delivery via TCS and Leopards Courier. Clients now span all major cities including Islamabad, Peshawar, and Faisalabad.
2023
Private Label & OEM
Introduced full private label manufacturing. Retailers and brands can now sell De-Shazeb quality under their own brand name.
2024
Online Store Launch
Launched official website and online shop. Over 50 products now available for retail and bulk ordering with secure checkout.
2025
International Expansion
Actively pursuing international export markets. Working towards partnerships with retailers and distributors across the Middle East and UK.
